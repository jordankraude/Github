Overview

Purpose

My goal for my website is to be a conventional portfolio for myself. I don’t have the goal to attract a lot of user’s to the page for an artificial or business reason. The main reason is to showcase my talents, skills, and knowledge that I have gained throughout my college education. As such I have a resume with my past job experience, I have some pages showing some of my ability to code javascript. Lastly, I have links from the main web pages to the important sites that employers would need to go to in order to see all the rest of my personal projects in python, C, Clojure, etc. I have ways for those who see the website to get back in touch with me if they are interested and or send me files. Later, when I have a personal server, I will expand this webpage to have some tools that others can come and use such as a file uploader and sender (my own personal). To summarize, shorterm; I see this webpage being more of a resume where only a select few will visit (mainly employers who I have given the link to). Long Term, as I can expand and add onto the webpage, then I can start making it more known so others will want to visit.

Main page

Why?

People will come to my site to see the many different tools and coding projects that I will add over time. My site will be almost like a personal github repository where it will showcase all of the different things that I can do and people will come to use those tools, send me ideas, or offer jobs. The main purpose of the site is to allow future employers to see my skill set and so they will come to view my showcase skills (shown through projects, etc.).


Questions

The main question that those who come to my site will be "what can I gain from here? Is there anything useful?" They will expect my site to answer those questions if I can be of use or if any of my projects can be of use.


No Competition

There isn't a concept of competition for my website. I am not trying to get people to my website to sell anything or show them ads that will make me money. The sole benefit of this website to me is the information that it can give me or the opportunities/jobs that it might bring me. So there is no reason why someone would choose this site over github. The people who come to my site will be those who have met me physically or seen my accounts on social media (Facebook, Instagram, Linkedin, etc.)
